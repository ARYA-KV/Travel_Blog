About Me



Hello, fellow wanderers!
I'm Heather , a spirited solo traveler hailing from the vibrant city of London. Embarking on exciting journeys across the globe, I've come to cherish the beauty of solo exploration and the boundless wonders that our world has to offer.My heart yearns for the thrill of discovering new cultures, immersing myself in local traditions, and savoring the unique flavors that each destination has to offer.My passion for travel goes beyond just visiting tourist spots; it's about delving into the heart and soul of each place, connecting with its people, and experiencing life in its most authentic form. As I navigate through the maze of cities, deserts, and mountains, I find joy in the shared laughter, stories, and friendships that transcend borders.Adventure awaits, and I can't wait to share it with you!

SEOUL & NEARBY

Every country has its major city, and for Korea that’s Seoul (which is also its
capital)! Located up towards the northwest part of the country, it’s easily one
of my favorite metropolitan areas in the whole world.
There’s just SO much going on no matter what you’re looking for, and it’s always changing.
It’s also very easy to see some cool nearby sights in Suwon and Gyeonggido, like a French storybook village, a kitschy island, a huge fortress, and more!
